Hi, I'm Ivan Mrekaj.

I am a data analyst and self-taught web app developer with environmental background. I enjoy developing apps and data visualisation. I studied phenology and I have worked as a climate scientist focused on changes in plant phenology under climate change conditions and drought occurrence and it's impact on forest ecosystems.

Download CV

Find some of my apps below.

Apps are built in Python, R Shiny, and JavaScript (Google Earth Engine). To visit the app click on the title or the image.

Phenological Trends

App built in R Shiny. App shows trends of selected forest trees and their phenolgical events.

Phenological Maps

This app is also build in R Shiny. App is dedicated to long-term phenological averages (phenological normals) and shows spatial occurence of phenological events.

Weather Forecast

A data web app build in Python that visualizes the weather forecast for today and the upcomming three days for any selected location. The app also plots air temperature, soil moisture and temperature, and pollen forecast.

Precipitation Inspector

Google Earth Engine web app (JavaScript), which shows the sum and time series of precipitation for selected time period for any location.
